Play Therapy

Play therapy is a gentle and evidence-based therapeutic approach that helps children express emotions, process experiences and develop healthy coping skills through play.

Since children often communicate feelings through play rather than words, therapy creates a safe and supportive space where healing, confidence and emotional growth can naturally unfold.

Healing Through the Language of Play

At Saarthi Navigating Life, we know that children often communicate through play long before they can fully express their emotions with words. A toy, a drawing, a story, or an imaginative game can reveal fears, worries, hopes, and feelings that may otherwise remain hidden.

Our Play Therapy sessions provide children with a safe, nurturing, and engaging environment where they can explore emotions, build confidence, develop healthy coping skills, and strengthen their emotional well-being through purposeful play. For children, play is more than entertainment—it is a natural language of expression, learning, and healing.
