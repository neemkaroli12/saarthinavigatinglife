Wellness Experts

Ms. Tulika Rastogi

Parent-Child Relationship Expert
With nearly two decades of experience in the field of education, I have dedicated my career to understanding, nurturing, and empowering children, parents, and families. Throughout my journey as an educator and school leader in India and abroad, I have witnessed countless stories of silent struggles—children misunderstood for their behaviour, parents overwhelmed by uncertainty, and individuals searching for someone who would simply listen without judgment.
These experiences inspired me to extend my work beyond the classroom and create a space where emotional well-being is valued just as much as academic and professional success.
I firmly believe that every individual has a story worth hearing and that healing begins when we feel truly seen, heard, and understood. My approach is rooted in empathy, patience, active listening, and practical guidance, helping people navigate life's challenges with confidence, resilience, and hope.
Whether I am supporting a child facing emotional or learning difficulties, guiding parents through the beautiful yet challenging journey of raising emotionally healthy and confident children, or simply being a compassionate companion for someone who feels lonely, anxious, or overwhelmed, my goal is always the same—to provide a safe, confidential, and non-judgmental space where people feel accepted, valued, and free to express themselves.
For me, SAARTHI – Navigating Life LLP is more than a Wellness Center; it is a reflection of my purpose and my belief that no one should have to face life's challenges alone. My mission is to walk alongside every individual with compassion, helping them rediscover hope, inner strength, emotional balance, and the courage to move forward—one meaningful conversation, one step, and one journey at a time.

Dr. Eishika Verma

Mental Health Specialist
I am a Mental Health and Wellness Specialist with a deep passion for empowering individuals, especially children, adolescents, and young adults, to lead emotionally healthy and fulfilling lives. Guided by both empathy and evidence-based practice, I strive to create meaningful and lasting positive change in the lives of those I work with.
With an advanced academic foundation, including a Ph.D. in Human Development and a Master's degree in Psychology, I have dedicated my career to understanding the intricate relationship between emotional well-being, personal growth, and human potential. My work involves designing holistic wellness frameworks and providing evidence-based therapeutic interventions tailored to the unique needs of each individual..
A significant part of my practice focuses on comprehensive psychological assessments that help uncover an individual's strengths, challenges, and emotional needs. I believe that every assessment is more than a diagnostic tool—it is an opportunity to understand a person's unique story and pave the way for emotional resilience, stronger social connections, improved self-awareness, and academic or personal success.
For me, mental health is not merely about overcoming challenges; it is about helping people discover their inner strengths, build healthy relationships, and develop the confidence to navigate life's complexities with resilience and purpose. I believe that every individual deserves to be heard, understood, and supported in a compassionate, non-judgmental environment.
Whether I am working with a child, guiding a teenager through life's transitions, supporting parents, or helping adults navigate emotional challenges, my goal remains the same—to provide a safe space where healing, growth, and self-discovery can flourish. I am committed to walking alongside every individual on their journey toward emotional well-being, because I believe that with the right support, every person has the capacity to heal, grow, and thrive.

Ms. Mahi Bisht

Art & Creative Wellness Enthusiast
I believe that healing often begins where words fall short. Through the power of creativity, art, and imagination, I help individuals express emotions that may be difficult to put into words, creating opportunities for self-discovery, healing, and personal growth.
As an Art and Creative Wellness Expert , I am passionate about creating a calm, nurturing, and non-judgmental space where children, adolescents, and adults feel safe to explore their emotions. I believe that every painting, every colour, every brushstroke, and every creative expression tells a story—one that deserves to be acknowledged and understood.
My approach is centred on the belief that creativity has the power to reduce stress, build confidence, encourage emotional resilience, and foster a deeper connection with oneself. Through art-based activities, mindful expression, storytelling, and imaginative exploration, I encourage individuals to embrace their emotions, discover their strengths, and develop healthy ways to cope with life's challenges.
For me, art is not about creating something perfect—it is about expressing what the heart feels and the mind cannot always explain. Every creative journey is unique, and I feel privileged to walk alongside individuals as they rediscover joy, confidence, and inner peace through self-expression.
At SAARTHI – Navigating Life LLP, my goal is to help every individual experience the therapeutic power of creativity. I aspire to inspire hope, emotional well-being, and personal growth by reminding people that healing is not a destination—it is a journey, and sometimes, all it takes is the courage to pick up a paintbrush and begin.
