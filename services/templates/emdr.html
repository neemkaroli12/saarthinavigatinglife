EMDR Therapy

Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR) is an evidence-based therapy that helps individuals heal from trauma, emotional distress and painful memories.

EMDR supports the brain in reprocessing overwhelming experiences, reducing emotional intensity and helping individuals feel calmer, safer and more emotionally balanced.

Healing Beyond Trauma

At Saarthi Navigating Life, we understand that some life experiences leave emotional wounds that continue to affect how we think, feel, and respond long after the event has passed.

Painful memories, trauma, grief, accidents, abuse, bullying, or distressing experiences can sometimes remain "stuck" in the brain, making it difficult to fully heal.

Our goal is not to erase your memories—but to help you remember them without feeling overwhelmed by them.

EMDR Process

A supportive and structured process designed to help you move from understanding difficult experiences toward emotional relief and healing.

01

Understand

Explore experiences, emotional triggers and therapy goals in a safe space.

02

Stabilize

Learn grounding tools and emotional regulation before deeper processing.

03

Reprocess

Use guided bilateral stimulation to safely process distressing memories.

04

Heal

Build emotional relief, healthier beliefs and a renewed sense of inner safety.
